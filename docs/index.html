About TK Munene Advocates

TK Munene Advocates specializes in succession, civil and criminal law, and provides land & estate consultancy, surveying and dispute resolution mechanisms. We carry out due diligence for both local and diaspora clients to prevent legal issues arising from lack of due diligence.
